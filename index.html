What equipment do I need for skiing?

Basic skiing equipment includes skis, boots, poles, and appropriate clothing. You can rent equipment on-site if you don't have your own.

Are there training programs for beginners?

Yes, we offer training programs for all skill levels, including beginners. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of skiing.

How do I book a private lesson?

You can book a private lesson by contacting our reservations team or using our online booking system. Choose your preferred date, time, and instructor.

What is ski touring?

Ski touring involves skiing in natural, unmarked terrain. It's an adventure where skiers ascend slopes using climbing skins and then ski back down.

How can I book skiing sessions or tours?

You can book online through our website or contact our reservations team. Provide your details, preferred dates, and any specific requirements.
